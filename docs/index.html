A notch utility that behaves like it belongs on the Mac.

Gojo is a small macOS app that opens from the notch and keeps frequent controls close without adding another permanent window to your desktop.

Music and media

Control Apple Music, Spotify, and browser audio with artwork, playback controls, and scrubbing inside the notch.

Window snapping

Move the focused window into common layouts with one click or keyboard shortcuts, routed through the helper that holds Accessibility permission.

Clipboard and file shelf

Search recent clipboard items, pin important entries, and stage files by dropping them into the notch.
